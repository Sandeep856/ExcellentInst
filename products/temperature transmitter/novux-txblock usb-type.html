NOVUS TXBLOCK USB


TxBlock-USB is a versatile head-mounted temperature transmitter supporting various sensors (thermocouples, Pt100, Pt1000, NTC, and 0-50 mV). It offers high accuracy with a linearized 4-20 mA output and adjustable range, featuring 2-point calibration for field tuning. Compliant with top electromagnetic standards, it ensures reliability in industrial environments. The built-in USB port enables easy configuration and fine-tuning during periodic calibration reviews.


  • Universal input accepts Thermocouples J, K, T, E, N, R, S, B and Pt100, Pt1000 and NTC RTD with programmable working range
  • Loop powered 4-20 mA output
  • Linearized 4 to 20 mA or 20 to 4 mA output
  • Cold junction compensation for thermocouples
  • Cable resistance compensation on 3 or 4 wires Pt100 connection
  • USB micro-B port allows direct PC or smartphone connection for configuration
  • Thermocouple and mV Typical Accuracy: ± 0.1 %
  • RTD Typical Accuracy: ± 0.1 % for Pt100 and Pt1000 and ± 0.3 °C for NTC
