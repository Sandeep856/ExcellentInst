NOVUS TXRAIL


The DIN rail mount temperature transmitter, TxRail-USB, offers USB configuration for input type, measurement range, output type, and calibration. It supports loop-powered 4-20mA or 0-10V output, ensuring versatility. Known for high accuracy and user-friendly operation, TxRail-USB excels in both basic and complex measuring applications.


  • Output: 4-20 mA or 20-4 mA at 2 wires with loop supply.
  • Pt100 at 2 and 3 wires and thermocouples with linearized output.
  • PC Configuration through TxConfig-USB interface.
  • Cold junction compensation for thermocouples
  • 12 to 35 Vdc (TxRail 4-20 mA)
  • 18 to 25 Vdc (TxRail 0-10 Vdc)
  • Accuracy: Pt100 and 0 - 50 mV ± 0,2% from max thermocouple ranges and ± 0,3% from max range.
  • Temperature effect: 0,003% from range / °C.
