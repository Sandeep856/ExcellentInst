About us

Excellent Instruments deals in wide variety of industry level electronics instrumentation products of renowned brands in a very competitive price range. Excellent Instruments is a well-established manufacturer of process control instruments with a strong technological foundation and a stellar reputation for producing high-end, cutting-edge instruments for the process control sector. We have been in this business since 2014, committed to provide high quality products and end to end sound customer satisfaction to our clients. Apart from products of other companies, we have our own product range and expertise in flameproof instruments and sensors which are customisable to customer needs. We majorly deal in instruments related to temperature controllers, PID controllers, temperature transmitters etc., sensors like temperature sensors, thermowell, gauges and automation. We provide our product support primarily to companies that function in pharmaceutical, chemical and glass instruments fields and have been our transmitted partners for over a decade. We have witnesses consistent growth over the years threrby gaining capability to scale our order capacity and manufacturing abilities. The above brands and companies are our major partners along with our own product range.
